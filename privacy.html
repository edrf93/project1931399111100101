Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes AdRyx.io's policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the Service and tells you about your privacy rights.

1. Definitions

For the purposes of this Policy, "Service" refers to the AdRyx.io website and the ad monetization platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information:

  • **Personal Data:** Includes name, email address, phone number, and website URL provided through the sign-up form.
  • **Usage Data:** Information collected automatically, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited on our site.
  • **Ad Data:** Non-personal data related to ad impressions, clicks, and revenue metrics.

3. Use of Your Personal Data

We may use Personal Data for the following purposes: to provide and maintain our Service, to manage your publisher application, to contact you regarding your account, and for business analytics.

4. Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We may share your personal information with service providers to monitor and analyze the use of our Service, or with business partners to offer you relevant products or services. We will never sell your contact information to third parties.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you.
